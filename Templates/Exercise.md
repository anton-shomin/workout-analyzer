# Description

Complex exercise: clean two kettlebells to chest from standing position, then thruster (squat + press overhead in one movement).

## Technique

1. Stand over kettlebells, feet shoulder-width apart
2. Take kettlebells in hands, perform clean to chest
3. Lower into squat with kettlebells on chest
4. Standing up, perform press of kettlebells overhead in one movement
5. Lower kettlebells to starting position
