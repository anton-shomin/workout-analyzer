Complex exercise combining squat with kettlebell clean to chest and overhead press.

Technique:

1. Starting position - kettlebell in lowered hand
2. Kettlebell pull to belt with simultaneous squat
3. At bottom - kettlebell clean to chest
4. Kettlebell press overhead
5. Lower kettlebell to starting position

Works: legs, shoulders, triceps, core
